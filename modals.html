Principal's Words

Welcome to the web-site of MD Seminary HSS Kottayam, Kerala, India. We hope that you will find the site informative. Of course, it would be impossible to convey the vitality and enthusiasm which permeate our school through any electronic medium. However, you will get information here about some of the activities and interests which make our school so special.

We salute the many thousands of students who are proud to call Dionisians. We love to hear from you and of you. We are particularly proud of so many who have become involved in helping the less fortunate in so many places and circumstances. You will be glad to know that this tradition is still nurtured and promoted in MD.

Those of you who attended this school for many years are also remembered with gratitude and affection.

We believe that “The only place where Success comes before Work is a Dictionary” & intend to implant the same in our students mind.

We here at MD believe in imparting quality education with deep rooted values which will enable students to meet the challenges of different facets of life.

To parents of present pupils and parents of prospective pupils - we hope that the web-site will provide you with easy access to information about the school and about the various events which take place in the school on an on-going basis. If you have any suggestions about data which might be included, we will be delighted to hear from you. Enjoy your browsing of the web!

Reji Abraham
M.Sc., B.Ed., M.Phil., P.G.D.C.A.
Principal, MD Seminary HSS, Kottayam

Goals

  • Imparting disciplined, liberal and comprehensive education to boys and girls alike to enable them to achieve a very high standard of intellectual, physical and spiritual development This is in the background of the rich cultural and spiritual heritage of our nation, so that they may discharge their duties as upright citizens of our Sovereign, Democratic and Secular Republic.
  • Creating self sufficient and self reliant generations who can in turn lead our nation to prosperity and development.
  • To focus on moral and ethics by imparting value based education.
  • Enhancing values of tolerance, generosity and harmony in the upcoming generations so as to develop universal brother hood.
  • Creating social consciousness, dignity of labour and concern for the down-trodden in the minds of the pupils, so that they may become instruments of social changes.
  • Scaffolding the youngsters to help them realize their talents and potentials which enable them to scale heights of brilliant careers.
  • Channelizing their energy in positive and creative manners.
  • Giving encouragement to exceptionally brilliant students from educationally and financially backward sections through schemes of scholarship and endowments.
